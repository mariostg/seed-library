Plant Profile Catalogue

Welcome to our Plant Profile Collection

Explore our comprehensive database of plant profiles, curated to help you identify and understand various plant species. Each profile provides detailed information about growth characteristics, habitat, and ecological significance, landscape uses, and more.

Use the filters and search options to find specific plants or browse through our categories to discover new species.

Click on any plant profile to view detailed information, images, and ecological data.

The Seed Library uses VASCAN to determine the correct names of species. We only accept species native to Ontario, as determined by VASCAN. As a result, the catalogue only includes plants that are determined by VASCAN as native to Ontario.
